Mount Isa Community Platform

A community-led approach to understanding and improving services on Kalkadoon Country

Why We Built This Platform

"Don't fly in and fly out and think you're going to solve problems. We need services that our people can trust and programs that are here when we need them."
— Roslyn Von Senden, Traditional Owner & Community Leader

For too long, remote communities like Mount Isa have been studied by outsiders rather than telling our own story. This platform represents a fundamental shift—putting community voices at the center of understanding our service needs and celebrating our strengths.

Built on Kalkadoon Country, this technology platform combines traditional Indigenous knowledge systems with cutting-edge AI to create the most comprehensive understanding of community services ever developed for our region. Every feature reflects our values: community-led, culturally-centered, and built to last.
