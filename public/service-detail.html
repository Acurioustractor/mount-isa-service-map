About This Service

headspace Mount Isa is part of Australia's National Youth Mental Health Foundation, providing free mental health services to young people aged 12-25 years in the Mount Isa and surrounding communities.

Our multidisciplinary team includes psychologists, social workers, mental health nurses, and peer workers who work together to provide holistic care tailored to each young person's needs.

How to Access

🚀 Quick Start

No referral required - you can walk in, call, or book online. Same-day appointments available for urgent concerns.

  1. Contact us: Call (07) 4743 3700 or visit our website
  2. Initial assessment: Brief phone or in-person discussion about your needs
  3. Appointment booking: Schedule your first session with appropriate clinician
  4. Ongoing support: Regular sessions and access to group programs

Important Information

Crisis Support:
If you're in immediate danger, call 000
For mental health crisis support:
• Lifeline: 13 11 14
• Kids Helpline: 1800 55 1800
• Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636

Privacy: All services are confidential. Information is only shared with your consent, except in circumstances where there are serious safety concerns.

Cost: Services are free for eligible young people with a Medicare card.
